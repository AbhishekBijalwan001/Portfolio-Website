My Approach

I always knew I was creative but I didn't know where I could use it the best. I enjoyed working and researching in different segments of a design project. I had ideas and with certain set of skills I could execute them fruitfully.
In order to enhance my skills and improve my level of execution and output, I know I need to learn the other aspects of this field. I want to develop skills that bring out my creativity in the best possible way and help me improve professionally. The course 'Interactive Media Management’ offer me just the things I was looking for.
I believe that in order to direct a creative project it's important to have in depth knowledge of everything that the field offers. My aim is to be a creative director in the field of advertising, so it's really important to learn and hone every skill.
